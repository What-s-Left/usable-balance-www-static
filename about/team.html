Mathew Stokes

CEO

Hey! I'm Stokes.

With over two decades of experience in SaaS leadership and commercial banking, I’ve honed my passion for building innovative solutions that drive customer satisfaction and operational excellence.

Drew Broadley

CPO

Kia ora, I'm Drew!

I have spent my past in financial businesses, starting with founding a paperless receipts business (acquired by Antipodean Labs, San Francisco, 2014), co-founding a common ledger accountants platform (exited 2017) and being CTO of a P2P lending product.

My experience over those ventures, along with many years of experience of being in leadership positions in organisations of all sizes - has led me to believe that the most important thing for a business is to know how much cash they have available to spend right now.

Jason Danner

COO

Hi! I'm Jason.

I've spent the last decade running a small technology company while working with businesses all over New Zealand and its painful to see otherwise successful businesses fail when their owners lose sight of the amount of money in their bank account that is actually safe to use.

Our goal is to make sure these businesses have the most up-to-date financial information they need to make informed decisions every day while requiring the least amount of effort from busy owners and managers.
