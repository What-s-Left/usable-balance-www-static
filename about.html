Why

We want to convert New Zealand businesses costs of $263.6 million in GST late fees & penalties paid to IRD and move that spend towards them purchasing using Usable Balance as an assurance to auto reconcile and file their GST for them, on time.

We want to decrease the 37.5% of businesses have received GST late fees & penalties in the last financial year, and turn that into a good experience for our customers on Usable Balance.

Source: OIA to IRD in November 2023 and July 2024 regarding GST late filing fees and business statistics
